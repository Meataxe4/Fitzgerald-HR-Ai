However, Fitz HR dramatically reduces the need for legal involvement by ensuring your process is correct from the start, generating compliant documentation, and preparing all documentation before you engage a lawyer — which significantly reduces legal costs when you do need one.

When a Traditional HR Consultant Still Makes Sense

  • Fair Work Commission representation: If you're defending an unfair dismissal claim at hearing, you need a lawyer or experienced advocate
