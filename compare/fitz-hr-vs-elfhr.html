The Depth Difference

Fitz HR serves one industry. Every AI response, every document, every piece of guidance is built around the Hospitality Industry (General) Award MA000009 and the Restaurant Industry Award. When you ask Fitz about evening loadings, it gives you the exact flat dollar loading for your classification level and shift type — not a general answer about penalty rates.

ElfHR is designed for all SMEs. That means its AI needs to serve the Retail Award, the Nursing Award, the Building and Construction Award, and dozens of others alongside hospitality. Breadth and depth are a trade-off. For a venue owner asking about a split shift allowance or a casual conversion obligation, the difference between a general answer and a hospitality-specific one can be the difference between compliance and a back-pay liability.

The Integration Advantage

ElfHR’s strength is integration. If you already use RosterElf for rostering and payroll, adding ElfHR keeps everything in one place — rosters, time tracking, payroll, and now digital HR tools. That’s a genuine convenience for venues already embedded in the RosterElf ecosystem.

Fitz HR is standalone by design — it works alongside RosterElf, Deputy, Tanda, or any other platform you use. Many venues choose both: RosterElf for operations, Fitz HR for compliance and expert guidance. They serve different functions and there is no conflict.

You don’t need to replace your existing systems. Fitz HR is designed to sit alongside your current setup — not disrupt it. For venues comparing RosterElf alternatives, it’s important to separate rostering from HR compliance — they solve different problems.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Fitz HR and ElfHR?
Fitz HR is a standalone hospitality-only HR compliance platform; ElfHR is an add-on to RosterElf designed for all SMEs. Fitz HR offers deeper Hospitality Award guidance, AI-generated documents, expert human review, and Crisis Mode. ElfHR offers basic HR tools integrated with RosterElf rostering and payroll. The two platforms serve different functions and can be used together.
Do I need RosterElf to use ElfHR?
Yes — ElfHR is an add-on for RosterElf customers and does not operate as a standalone product. Fitz HR is standalone and works alongside any rostering or payroll platform you already use, including RosterElf.
Can I use both Fitz HR and RosterElf?
Yes — many hospitality venues use both. Use RosterElf for rostering, time tracking, and payroll. Use Fitz HR for HR compliance guidance, AI-generated warning letters, and expert professional support when issues arise. The platforms are complementary, not competing.
Is ElfHR built for Australian hospitality?
ElfHR is built for Australian SMEs broadly, not exclusively for hospitality. It serves all industries. Fitz HR is hospitality-only, with AI trained specifically on the Hospitality Industry (General) Award MA000009 and Restaurant Industry Award. The depth of hospitality-specific guidance differs significantly between the two.
Does Fitz HR do rostering?
No — Fitz HR is focused on HR compliance, not rostering or payroll. For rostering, use a dedicated platform. Fitz HR works alongside RosterElf, Deputy, Tanda, or any other system you already have in place.
