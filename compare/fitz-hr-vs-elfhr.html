Fitz HR vs ElfHR (by RosterElf): Which Hospitality HR Tool Is Right for You?

16 Mar 2026 · By Fitz HR · 6 min read · Comparison ElfHR RosterElf

ElfHR is a new HR add-on launched by RosterElf, the Australian rostering platform. Fitz HR is a standalone AI-powered HR compliance platform built exclusively for hospitality. Both target Australian hospitality venues, but they take fundamentally different approaches.

Where Fitz HR Is Different

Hospitality-only focus: Fitz HR serves one industry. Every feature, every AI response, and every document is built for the Hospitality Industry (General) Award and the Restaurant Industry Award. ElfHR is designed for all SMEs — hospitality, retail, healthcare, childcare — which means it needs to serve many different Awards and industries. That breadth comes at the cost of depth.

AI-generated documents, not templates: Fitz HR's Document Builder generates documents based on your specific situation. You describe the issue, and the AI produces a customised warning letter, PIP, or contract. ElfHR provides digital contracts and policies, which is closer to a template library.

Human expert backstop: Every Fitz HR document can be reviewed by a qualified HR consultant before you use it. Crisis Mode connects you with a real human for urgent situations. This is a core differentiator that ensures AI-generated content is validated by professionals.

Where ElfHR Has an Advantage

Rostering integration: If you already use RosterElf for rostering and payroll, ElfHR adds HR tools within the same platform. Everything is in one place — rosters, time tracking, payroll, and now HR. Fitz HR is a standalone tool that works alongside your existing rostering platform.

Digital onboarding: ElfHR includes digital onboarding workflows integrated with the RosterElf employee database. Fitz HR provides onboarding guidance and document generation but does not manage the onboarding workflow itself.

The Verdict

If you want the deepest possible HR compliance coverage specifically for hospitality, with expert human review and crisis support, Fitz HR is the specialist choice. If you already use RosterElf and want basic HR tools added to your existing rostering workflow, ElfHR is a convenient add-on. Many venues may benefit from using both — RosterElf for rostering and basic HR administration, and Fitz HR for compliance guidance, document generation, and expert support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Fitz HR and ElfHR?

Fitz HR is a standalone AI-powered HR compliance platform built exclusively for Australian hospitality. ElfHR is an add-on to the RosterElf rostering platform designed for all SMEs. Fitz HR offers deeper hospitality-specific guidance, AI-generated documents, expert human review, and Crisis Mode. ElfHR offers HR tools integrated with rostering and payroll.

Do I need RosterElf to use ElfHR?

Yes. ElfHR is an optional add-on for RosterElf customers. Fitz HR is standalone and does not require any other platform — it works alongside whatever rostering or payroll system you already use.

Can I use both Fitz HR and RosterElf?

Yes. Many hospitality venues use a rostering platform like RosterElf, Deputy, or Tanda for scheduling and payroll, and Fitz HR for HR compliance, document generation, and expert guidance. The two platforms are complementary.
