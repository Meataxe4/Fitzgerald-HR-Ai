What to look for

For a childcare service, generic HR software is the wrong tool for the most expensive risk you carry: getting the award wrong. When you compare options, weigh these first:

  • Award-grounded answers — does it cite the Children's Services Award (MA000120), or give generic guidance?
  • Correct rates — exact pay, penalties and allowances, kept current at the annual wage review.
  • Award-correct documents — contracts and warnings that reflect Children's Services Award rules, not a generic template.
  • Speed in a crisis — immediate, grounded steps when something goes wrong on shift.
  • Transparent price — no implementation project or per-seat surprises.
