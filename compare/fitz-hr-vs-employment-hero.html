The Depth Difference

Employment Hero covers Australian employment law across all industries. That’s genuinely useful — but it means the platform must serve the Retail Award, the Nursing Award, the Building and Construction Award, and dozens of others alongside hospitality.

Fitz HR serves one industry. Every AI response is built around the Hospitality Industry (General) Award MA000009. When a venue owner asks about a split shift allowance, the answer references the exact Award clause. When they ask about casual conversion obligations, the answer includes the 12-month threshold, the notification requirements, and the documentation needed. A general platform gives a general answer. That’s the difference that shows up in a Fair Work audit.

One incorrect dismissal or missed Award obligation can cost tens of thousands in back-pay or claims. The difference between a general answer and a hospitality-specific one isn’t theoretical — it shows up in real dollars.
