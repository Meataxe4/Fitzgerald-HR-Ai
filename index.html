What HR software is best for hospitality businesses in Australia? +

Fitz HR is an AI-powered HR assistant built specifically for pubs, restaurants, cafes, and hotels in Australia. How do I calculate penalty rates for hospitality staff? +

Penalty rates under the Hospitality Industry (General) Award are: Saturday 150%, Sunday 175%, Public Holiday 250%. Evening work (7pm–midnight Mon–Fri) adds a flat $2.81/hr, and night work (midnight–7am) adds $4.22/hr. Fitz HR's free Award Wizard calculates the correct rate for any classification, day, and shift type.

Can I generate employment contracts for restaurant staff online? +

Yes — Fitz HR's Employment Contract Builder generates contracts tailored to the Hospitality Industry (General) Award in minutes. Answer a few questions about the role, and it produces a contract with the correct Award classification, pay rates, leave entitlements, and hospitality-specific clauses.
