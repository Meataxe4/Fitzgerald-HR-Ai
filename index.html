How do I calculate penalty rates for hospitality staff?
Penalty rates under the Hospitality Industry (General) Award are: Saturday 150%, Sunday 175%, Public Holiday 250%. Evening work (7pm–midnight Mon–Fri) adds a flat $2.81/hr loading, and night work (midnight–7am) adds $4.22/hr. Fitz HR's Award Wizard calculates the correct rate for any classification, day, and shift type.
