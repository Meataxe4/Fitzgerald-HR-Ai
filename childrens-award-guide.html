The complete reference to the Children's Services Award MA000120 for long day care, preschools, kindergartens and outside-school-hours care providers — coverage, classifications, penalty rates, allowances, minimum engagement and Fair Work compliance.

What the Children's Services Award Covers

The Children's Services Award MA000120 covers long day care, preschools and kindergartens, outside-school-hours care, and other children’s services. Getting coverage right matters — it drives the classification structure, penalty rates, and allowances that apply to every shift. Applying the wrong award creates systematic underpayment exposure.

Penalty Rates

Penalty rates under MA000120 for weekend and public holiday work, as a percentage of the ordinary hourly rate:

When worked% of ordinary rate
Saturday (FT/PT)150%
Saturday (casual)175%
Sunday (FT/PT)200%

Public holiday (FT/PT)250%

Casual employees receive a 25% loading; the casual penalty percentages above are all-inclusive of that loading, not stacked on top.

Minimum Engagement

The Children's Services Award sets a minimum period an employee must be paid for each time they are engaged:

  • part time hours per shift: 2 hours minimum per shift
  • casual hours per shift: 2 hours minimum per shift

Fair Work Compliance

Operating under MA000120 creates obligations beyond paying the correct rate:

  • Record keeping: keep employee records for 7 years, including actual (not rostered) start and finish times, classification, rates, allowances and superannuation.
  • Payslips: issue within one working day of pay day with all required information.
  • Casual Employment Information Statement: provide at commencement and at the relevant conversion milestone.
  • Superannuation: currently 12% on ordinary time earnings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Children's Services Award MA000120?
It is a modern award under the Fair Work Act 2009 (Cth) that sets minimum pay rates, penalty rates, allowances and conditions. It covers long day care, preschools and kindergartens, outside-school-hours care, and other children’s services.
What are the penalty rates under MA000120 in 2026?
Saturday 150% and Sunday 200% for full-time and part-time employees, with a public holiday rate of 250%. Casual rates are all-inclusive of the 25% loading. See the full pay rates page.
Which award covers long day care and OSHC?
The Children's Services Award MA000120 covers long day care, preschools and kindergartens, and outside-school-hours care. Teachers in early childhood settings may instead be covered by the relevant education teachers award — Fitz confirms coverage first.
How current are these Children's Services Award figures?
They are extracted from the Fair Work Ombudsman Pay Guide MA000120, current as at 2026-07-01, with the next review due 2027-07-01.
