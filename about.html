About Fitz HR

AI-Powered HR Compliance Software for Hospitality Businesses in Australia —
Built for the Moments That Matter Most

Fitz HR is an AI-powered HR compliance platform designed specifically for Australian hospitality businesses. It helps restaurant, bar, cafe, pub, and hotel operators stay compliant with the Fair Work Act 2009 (Cth) and Hospitality Award MA000009 by providing instant guidance, compliant document generation, and on-demand access to qualified HR professionals — 24/7. For venues without a dedicated HR team, it replaces the need for an external HR consultant for all but the most complex legal proceedings.

Hospitality HR doesn’t fail at 9am in a meeting room. It fails at 10pm on a Saturday when a manager makes a call without the right information — no documented warning, no fair process, no paper trail. Fitz HR exists for that moment. And every routine compliance decision before it.

Built by hospitality operators and HR professionals with real-world experience in Australian Fair Work compliance, workplace disputes, and Award interpretation — for venues that can’t afford to get it wrong.

Common Questions
What is Fitz HR?
Fitz HR is an AI-powered HR compliance platform built exclusively for Australian hospitality venues. It provides instant guidance on the Hospitality Industry (General) Award MA000009 and the Fair Work Act 2009, generates compliant employment documents, and connects venue operators with qualified HR professionals for complex disputes and crisis situations. It is not a generic HR platform adapted for hospitality — it was built specifically for pubs, restaurants, cafes, bars, and hotels operating under Australian employment law.
Does Fitz HR cover the Hospitality Industry (General) Award?
Yes — it is built specifically around the Hospitality Industry (General) Award MA000009 and Restaurant Industry Award MA000119. Every AI response is aligned to classification levels, the full penalty rate schedule (Saturday 125%/150%, Sunday 150%/175%, Public Holiday 225%/250% — full-time-or-part-time vs casual), evening loadings ($2.81/hr), night loadings ($4.22/hr), casual loading, and overtime provisions under these Awards. Is Fitz HR a replacement for an HR consultant?
For most hospitality venues, yes. Fitz HR combines instant AI guidance for routine HR questions with direct access to qualified HR professionals for complex investigations, disputes, and crisis situations.
