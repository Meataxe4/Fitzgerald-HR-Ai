Best HR Software for Hospitality in Australia (2026)

Published 22 February 2026 · By Fitz HR · 8 min read

If you run a pub, restaurant, cafe, or hotel in Australia, you know HR is one of the most stressful parts of the job. Between penalty rates, Fair Work compliance, casual employment rules, and managing performance issues, hospitality HR is significantly more complex than most other industries.

Most HR software is built for corporate offices, not venues. This guide compares the tools that actually work for Australian hospitality, with a focus on compliance with the Hospitality Industry (General) Award and Fair Work Act.

What to Look for in Hospitality HR Software

Before comparing tools, here’s what matters most for hospitality venues:

  • Australian Award compliance: Does it understand the Hospitality Award, penalty rates, and classification levels?
  • Document generation: Can it produce employment contracts, warning letters, and PIPs that reference the correct Award?
  • HR guidance: Can it answer specific questions about Fair Work requirements for hospitality?
  • Rostering & time tracking: Does it handle the complexities of hospitality scheduling?
  • Price point: Is it affordable for a single-venue operation?

Best for: Staff rostering and time tracking for clubs and hospitality venues.

ClubsHR is a Sydney-based platform originally built for the clubs industry (RSLs, leagues clubs, etc.) and also used by hospitality venues. Its core strength is rostering with built-in award interpretation for payroll, making it ideal for venues where scheduling is the primary challenge.

Deputy — Workforce Management

Best for: Shift scheduling, time tracking, and team communication.
