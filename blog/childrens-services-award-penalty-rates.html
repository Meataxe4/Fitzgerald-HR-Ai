Children's Services Award Penalty Rates

1 Jul 2026 Updated 1 Jul 2026 By Fitz HR 5 min read Reviewed against FWO Pay Guide MA000120

Weekend and public holiday work is less common in early childhood than hospitality, but it happens — and the rates are high. This guide sets out the exact Children's Services Award MA000120 penalty rates for 2026.

Under MA000120, Saturday is 150% (175% casual), Sunday 200% (225% casual) and public holidays 250% (275% casual). Casual percentages already include the 25% loading. Shift loadings apply for early-morning (10%), afternoon (15%), rotating night (17.5%) and permanent night (30%) work.

Rates current as at 2026-07-01 (Annual Wage Review), sourced from the Fair Work Ombudsman Pay Guide MA000120. Next review 2027-07-01.

Children's Services Penalty Rates 2026

Saturday: 150% (FT/PT) · 175% (casual)
Sunday: 200% (FT/PT) · 225% (casual)
Public holiday: 250% (FT/PT) · 275% (casual)
Shift loadings: early-morning +10%, afternoon +15%, night +17.5% / +30%

Worked Examples

For a Support Worker Level 1.1 on $26.44/hr:

  • Saturday: $39.66/hr (FT/PT) · $46.27/hr (casual)
  • Sunday: $52.88/hr (FT/PT) · $59.49/hr (casual)
  • Public holiday: $66.10/hr (FT/PT) · $72.71/hr (casual)

Shift Loadings

Shift loadings apply to ordinary hours: early-morning 10%, afternoon 15%, rotating night 17.5% and permanent night 30%. On weekends and public holidays the penalty rate applies instead — the higher single entitlement, not both.

Common Mistakes That Trigger Audits

✗
Paying Sunday rates on public holidays. Public holidays are 250%/275%.
✗
Stacking casual loading on the casual penalty rate. The casual percentages already include the 25% loading.
✗
Stacking a shift loading on a weekend penalty. The higher single entitlement applies, not both.
