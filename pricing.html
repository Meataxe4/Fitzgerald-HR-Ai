Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits used for?

Credits are your currency for Fitz services. Use them for: Document Generation (contracts, position descriptions, onboarding docs), Expert-Reviewed Documents (warnings, PIPs, terminations reviewed by qualified HR consultants before you use them), and HR Consultations (book time with professionals for any workplace matter). Different services use different amounts of credits.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing date.

What if I need more credits?

You can purchase additional credits at any time. Extra credits are $29 each, or 5 for $119. They never expire and roll over year to year.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free tier with limited access so you can try Fitz before committing. Plus, all paid plans come with a 14-day money-back guarantee ‚Äî if it's not right for you, we'll refund your payment.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure Australian servers, and never share your data with third parties. Your HR conversations and documents are private and protected.
