What's included in "expert-reviewed documents"?

Every formal HR document you generate (warning letters, PIPs, termination letters, contracts) can be reviewed by a qualified HR consultant before you use it. They'll check for compliance, completeness, and appropriateness for your situation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing date.
