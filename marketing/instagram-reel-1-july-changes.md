## Caption (post body)

**It's 1 July — here's what changed for hospitality & restaurants 👇**

1️⃣ **Award pay rates rise 4.75%** from the first full pay period on or after 1 July 2026 — penalties, loadings and overtime rise with the base.
2️⃣ **Payday super** — you must pay super at the same time as wages, with contributions in the employee's fund within 7 business days (20 for new starters).
3️⃣ **Penalty rates are locked in** — the Fair Work Commission can no longer cut penalty or overtime rates under any modern award.
4️⃣ **Compliance scrutiny stays high** — hospitality is a regular Fair Work Ombudsman target; guessing rates or copying the café next door is real underpayment risk.
5️⃣ **Leave & casual rules** — government-funded Paid Parental Leave rises to 26 weeks, and casuals with a regular pattern can give written notice to convert to permanent under the new "employee choice" rules.
